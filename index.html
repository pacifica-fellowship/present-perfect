Who We Are

Three Decades of
Beverly Hills Gifting

A company that has been around for three decades, serving Beverly Hills premium gift baskets for the holidays and special occasions throughout the year. Present Perfect delivers superior customer service, and tailor-made experiences for each person, at a high level that has earned us distinction in our field. Started by Barbara Kalmus, the business has flourished under her children, Perry, Dari and Ori Kalmus. Present Perfect is the best choice to make your present no less than perfect.
